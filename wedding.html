Getting there?

There’s Cork City airport (ORK) which is a convenient 15 min drive to Cork city center. It also has the best airport art installation you’ll ever see, Redhead Exhibition showcasing various portraits of people with ginger hair.

Irish Airport

Flights to this airport tend to be a bit pricier than flights to….

Dublin Airport (DUB) usually has cheaper flights and would be a good option for those extending their trip to see more of Ireland. Dublin is a 3-hour drive or very inexpensive and easy 3-hour bus trip using GoBus.ie. Think of it as seeing the scenery as you arrive.
